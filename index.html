Who are we?

ILS is a charity, based in Dumfries, we were formed 2002 we have many years of experience providing outreach housing support to individuals in the community. We offer support to clients from 16 upwards who are homeless or at risk of homelessness. Our aim is to encourage and support an individual’s journey toward increased independence. As our mission statement expresses our aim for those who use our service. To go from:

"From simply surviving to actively thriving"
